## Hello, I’m Josh 👋

Welcome to my little corner of the internet.

I’m a web developer with a passion for building fast, thoughtful, and user-friendly experiences on the web. This site is a space for me to share what I’m learning, what I’m building, and what I’m thinking about as I continue to grow in this field.

## What you’ll find here

I’ll be using this platform to post a mix of:

- **Tutorials**: practical guides on web development, tools, and workflows
- **Posts**: updates on projects I’m working on and things I’m experimenting with
- **Thoughts**: ideas, opinions, and reflections on the web, tech, and the industry

Some posts might be polished, others a bit more rough and exploratory, but all of them will be genuine.

## Why this site?

I wanted a space that’s truly my own. No algorithms, no noise just a simple, fast, and focused place to share ideas and connect with others who are interested in the same things.

It’s also a way for me to document my journey. Writing things down helps me think more clearly, and hopefully it’ll be useful to someone else along the way too.

## What’s next?

I’ve got a few ideas lined up already from lightweight site setups and tooling, to building small SaaS ideas and improving developer workflows.

If that sounds like your kind of thing, stick around.

Thanks for stopping by.
