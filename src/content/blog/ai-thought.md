I kept thinking about this for a while and decided to remove chatgpt and ai tool from my phine to encourage my researching brain to work harder as I think relying on AI to do my thinking is giving away to much of my own power.

I will still use it appropriately within my work as it helps structure and debug my thoughts processes for coding but general day to day the old grey cells brain is gonna work!!
